Empowering Brands That Shape the Market.

Orderintel
OrderIntel is an advanced AI Restaurant Management System (RMS) designed to give restaurants complete visibility and control over their operations. Using powerful computer vision, the platform transforms ordinary cameras into intelligent monitors that automate object tracking, violation detection, and real-time environment analysis. Instead of relying on manual supervision, OrderIntel ensures accurate product counts, consistent safety compliance, and a healthier, more efficient workspace.
Our mission is to help restaurants operate smarter by eliminating hidden inefficiencies and reducing operational risks. Whether managing a single outlet or multiple locations, OrderIntel delivers live insights, instant alerts, and data-driven recommendations that enhance performance and protect brand standards. With OrderIntel, restaurants achieve higher productivity, improved safety, and an optimized, future-ready dining experience.

OrderIntel uses advanced computer vision to monitor every item in your kitchen and dining workflow. From ingredient movement to equipment usage, the system automatically generates accurate product counts, identifies misplaced items, and tracks operational flow in real time. This helps reduce waste, prevent shortages, and maintain consistency across shifts without relying on manual checks or staff supervision.

Our system continuously detects safety, hygiene, and compliance violations, ensuring your restaurant meets industry standards at all times. Whether it's missing gloves, improper food handling, blocked exits, or unattended stations, OrderIntel instantly flags issues and sends alerts. This proactive monitoring protects your brand, reduces risk, and ensures every team member follows the right procedures.

OrderIntel creates a healthy, efficient, and high-performing environment by analyzing crowd flow, service delays, workstation cleanliness, and overall operational conditions. The platform identifies bottlenecks, predicts risks, and recommends improvements, empowering managers to make data-driven decisions. With real-time dashboards and actionable insights, restaurants can enhance guest experience, speed of service, and overall operational quality.

Services

AI Object Tracking

OrderIntel uses advanced computer vision to automatically track ingredients, tools, and equipment across your kitchen and dining areas. It delivers accurate product counts, identifies misplaced items, and monitors workflow movement in real time, reducing waste, preventing shortages, and improving overall operational consistency.

Violation Detection & Safety Compliance

Our system continuously monitors hygiene, safety, and operational standards. From missing gloves to unattended stations or improper food handling, OrderIntel instantly detects violations and sends real-time alerts, helping you maintain strict compliance, avoid risks, and ensure a safe environment for staff and guests.

Healthy Environment Monitoring

OrderIntel analyzes crowd flow, cleanliness, service delays, and workstation conditions to maintain a healthy, efficient environment. It identifies bottlenecks, predicts risks, and provides actionable insights, helping managers improve service speed, staff performance, and the overall guest experience.

Real-Time Performance Analytics

Gain complete operational visibility with live dashboards and intelligent reporting. OrderIntel delivers real-time insights on productivity, safety, and workflow efficiency, enabling data-driven decisions. Managers can optimize processes, track improvements, and maintain consistent performance across single or multi-location restaurants.

Do we need new cameras to use OrderIntel? −
No, you can use your existing IP or CCTV cameras. Our system supports most standard protocols, and we’ll determine compatibility during onboarding.
How accurate is the object tracking/violation detection? +
Our computer-vision models are trained on millions of frames and customized per location. We regularly benchmark performance; typical accuracy rates exceed 95% for object recognition and violation detection.
Is this only for the kitchen, or can it monitor dining areas too +
Both. OrderIntel tracks back-of-house operations (inventory, prep, staff) as well as front-of-house activity (guest flow, table occupancy, staff service).
How is data accessed, and what about security? +
You access your dashboard through a secure web portal or mobile app. All video and metadata are encrypted both at rest and in transit. We comply with industry-standard security practices.
Can this scale to multiple locations? +
Yes. The system is cloud-enabled with multi-tenant support, centralised analytics, and standardised metrics across all sites.
